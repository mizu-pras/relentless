description: "Browse archived pursuit history. Use '--detail <id>' for specific pursuit details, '--stats' for aggregate statistics."
---

Arguments: $ARGUMENTS

Load the `relentless:history` skill and follow its instructions.

Pass arguments to the skill:
- No arguments or `--list`: show all archived pursuits
- `--detail <id>`: show details of a specific archived pursuit
- `--stats`: show aggregate statistics across all pursuits
